Privacy Policy – xPredictors

Last updated: 2025-10-01

Who we are

xPredictors (“the App”) is a community-based app where users can make daily stock predictions (closing price and direction) and view aggregated results and statistics.

What data we collect

  • Prediction data: votes for daily stock predictions (Bull/Bear, price guesses) and optional X (Twitter) handle.
  • Device identifiers: anonymous IDFV and Firebase-generated UID, used to limit abuse and manage votes.
  • App integrity tokens: Firebase App Check tokens to verify genuine app usage and prevent fraud.
  • Analytics/Ads data: collected automatically by Google AdMob for ad delivery and performance measurement (may include device identifiers and cookies).
  • Purchase data: in-app purchases (“tips”) are processed securely by Apple; the App does not receive your full payment information.

How we use data

  • Record and count votes, generate leaderboards, and display community statistics.
  • Prevent fraudulent activity using App Check and device identifiers.
  • Serve and measure advertising (Google AdMob).
  • Process in-app purchases (Apple In-App Purchase).
  • Comply with App Store and legal requirements.

Where we store data

Prediction and vote data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore (cloud database). App integrity validation is handled by Firebase App Check. Authentication is handled by Firebase Auth. Ad/analytics data is processed by Google AdMob. Purchase records are managed by Apple.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only with trusted third-party providers strictly as needed:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore, App Check, Authentication)
  • Google AdMob
  • Apple (In-App Purchase, iOS services)

Retention

Votes may be retained for community statistics. Older records may be aggregated or deleted periodically. Identifiers may be stored as long as needed for fraud prevention and vote integrity.

Your choices

  • You can limit personalized ads in iOS system settings.
  • You may submit predictions without providing an X handle.
  • You may request deletion of your vote data by contacting us with your device details (IDFV or Firebase UID).

Children

This App is not directed to children under 13. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it.

Security

We rely on platform-level security provided by Apple and Google. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
