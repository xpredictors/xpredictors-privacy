Privacy Policy – xPredictors

Last updated: 2025-09-03

Who we are
xPredictors (“the App”) lets users submit daily closing-price predictions for the stock and view community statistics.

What data we collect

  • Prediction data: selected price and optional X (Twitter) handle.
  • Device identifier: anonymous IDFV to limit abuse and aggregate votes.
  • Analytics/Ads data: collected by Google AdMob for ad delivery and measurement.
  • Purchase data: handled by Apple; we do not receive full payment info.

How we use data

  • Record votes, compute averages/leaderboards, prevent spam, improve the App.
  • Show and measure advertising (Google AdMob).
  • Comply with legal and App Store requirements.

Where we store data

Prediction data is stored in Apple CloudKit (Public DB). Ad/analytics data is processed by Google AdMob.

Data sharing

No sale of personal data. Shared only with service providers necessary to operate the App.

Retention

Votes may be retained; older records may be aggregated or deleted periodically.

Your choices

  • Limit personalized ads in iOS settings.
  • Submit predictions without an X handle.
  • Deletion requests: contact us with device details (IDFV).

Children

Not directed to children under 13.

Security

We rely on platform security, but no method is 100% secure.
